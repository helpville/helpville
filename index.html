About Us

What is HelpVille Tutoring? We are a team of dedicated goal-oriented tutors offering unique tutoring sessions to Grade 7 up until Grade 9 students. Serving Calgary communities, we teach Science, Mathematics, English, Social Studies, and French following the Alberta curriculum. What sets our tutoring services apart from the rest? Our services are accessible to communities in the southwest and our tutors offer private lessons in student homes. We offer personalized academic plans that capitalize on the student's learning style, ability, and availability to yield effective results. We are very results-oriented which lends to the high-quality education the student receives.

The student's needs are catered to by our assessments. Our services are flexible as we recognize one size doesn't fit all and this applies to education. Our assessments help us effectively identify the student's strengths and weaknesses. We truly believe that students can achieve anything they set their minds to but a sizeable amount of students are overwhelmed with impersonal teaching strategies that do little to enable their growth. At HelpVille, we are passionate about helping students who require more specialized, student-centric services to realize their full potential.
